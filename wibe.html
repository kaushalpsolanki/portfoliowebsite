Mission Statement

Clip (previously Wibe) focuses on connecting people from the tier 2 and tier 3 cities of India by giving them a platform to watch, create and share minute long custom videos in their regional langugae. The App supports more than 10 regional Indian langugae apart from English

Task

To build the entire product from scratch using User-Centered Design Methodology while keeping business goals in mind

Solution

This mobile app that helps people around the country including tier 2 and 3 cities to make and follow different communities by the means of a minute long video in a non overwhelming way. Here are some of the key features that I came up with

Methodology

User Research

I reached out to students from 10-12th grade as well as local college students to ask their interest in acting, perfroming or dancing if given an opportunity and to test out the initial low wireframes and ideas. All the conversations were done face to face. The answers helped me design validate the hypothesis that the team formed about the features that were most important and it helped to corrent some of my design ideas.
